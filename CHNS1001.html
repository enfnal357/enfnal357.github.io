CHNS1001 Elementary Chinese

Aim of the Course

The aim of this elementary course is to develop our proficiency in modern Chinese and to help them prepare for intermeiate Chinese courses in the following academic year, and for further Chinese-related studies and/or jobs in the future. To support students to acquire fundamental language ability in Chinese the emphasis of this course will equally be placed in listening, speaking, reading and writing in various contexts, as well as a basic understanding of the Chinese grammer.In class, our learning will be focused on listening and speaking practices by participating in listening comprehension exercises, group dialogue exercises, personal presentations, etx. After class, we will be required to complete assignments both in character book and workbook as a means to enhance reading and writing skills.
